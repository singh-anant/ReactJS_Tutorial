Q1>What is CDN?
COntent Delivery Network-A place where our react is hosted.
A content delivery network (CDN) is a geographically distributed group of
servers that caches content close to end users. A CDN allows for the 
quick transfer of assets needed for loading Internet content, including 
HTML pages, JavaScript files, stylesheets, images, and videos.


Q2>What is crossorigin?
The term "cross-origin" refers to interactions between web pages from different origins. An origin is defined by the combination of a scheme (such as HTTP or HTTPS), domain, and port. When a web page makes a request to a different origin than the one that served the web page, 
it is considered a cross-origin request.
